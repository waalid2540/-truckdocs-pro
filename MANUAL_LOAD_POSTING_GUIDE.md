## Pro Tips for Speed

### 1. Focus on High-Value Loads
- Prioritize loads with rate per mile > $2.00
- Skip loads with sketchy broker info
- Focus on equipment types your drivers use most (probably Dry Van)

### 2. Batch Similar Routes
- Copy 10 loads from CA â†’ TX
- Then 10 loads from TX â†’ FL
- Pattern recognition makes you faster

### 3. Use Keyboard Shortcuts
- Tab between fields
- Don't fill optional fields if DAT doesn't show them
- The more loads, the better (quantity > perfection at this stage)

### 4. Update Daily
- Spend 30 minutes each morning posting 20-30 fresh loads
- Delete old loads (pickup date passed) once per week
- This keeps your board looking active and fresh

## Common Issues & Solutions

### "I can't find good loads on DAT"
- Filter by pickup date 2-7 days out
- Try different origin states (TX, CA, FL, GA have most loads)
- Lower your minimum rate filter temporarily

### "This is taking too long"
- Skip optional fields for now
- Focus on required fields only
- Speed will come with practice (10 loads in 20 minutes after day 3)

### "Loads are expiring before drivers book them"
- Normal at first - you need more drivers using platform
- Focus on marketing while posting loads
- Post loads with pickup dates 3-7 days out (not 1-2 days)

### "Should I post loads for all equipment types?"
- Start with Dry Van only (80% of drivers)
- Add Reefer/Flatbed once you have 50+ Dry Van loads
- Specialization > variety at this stage
